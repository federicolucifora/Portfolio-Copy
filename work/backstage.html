Curse of The Blind Dead Backstage
Photography | Reportage
Curse of The Blind Dead is an independent horror film produced by Mafarka Films and directed by Raffaele Picchio. My job was to take care of the backstage of the film, for which I took photos during the filming to have a report of what was going on behind the scenes.
The main location where the film was shot, the abandoned Italcementi cement factory in Alzano, in the province of Bergamo, was also particularly fascinating. This allowed me to create very suggestive shots that would capture the state of decay of the building, perfectly consistent with the atmosphere of the film. This experience was an inspiration for the realization of another project featured in this portfolio: “L’abbandono della Val Seriana”.
