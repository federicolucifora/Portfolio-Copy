L'abbandono della Val Seriana
Reportage | Photography | Storytelling
“L’abbandono della Val Seriana” is a photographic reportage that aims to give value to the abandoned factories and establishments located in Val Seriana, following the line of the “Tram delle Valli” that connects Bergamo and Albino. All these buildings could be transformed into points of historical interest in the field of industrial archeology. The tram would also be a convenient connection between the various structures, since all the places explored are easily reachable on foot from the respective stops. Through the reportage “L’abbandono della Val Seriana” I wanted to show the potential of those buildings that, at least for now, remain at the mercy of nature.
