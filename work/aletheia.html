Aletheia Photo Mosaic
Web Development | User Experience | Digital Interaction
Aletheia Photo Mosaic is a web browser-based digital experience created to engage fans of rap singer Izi in promoting his album “Aletheia”.
Collaborators
Lorenzo Barilla
Elisa Manzoni
Matilde Teani
Lorenzo Barilla, Elisa Manzoni, Matilde Teani
Through the platform it was be possible, from a computer or smartphone, to take a picture through the webcam which was then be uploaded to an online database. The photos were then used as tiles to generate a mosaic that recreated the image of the album cover.
People were be encouraged to upload new images so that they could see the mosaic in its entirety as soon as possible, and by accessing the “Watch the mosaic” section it's possible to observe the completion status of the mosaic at the time of navigation. Furthermore, it is also possible to zoom into the mosaic so as to be able to distinguish the single images that make it up.
