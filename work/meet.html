MEETverso
3D Modeling | Space Design
MEETverso is a virtual environment created by Ultra Srl and commissioned by the MEET Digital Culture Center with the contribution of the city of Milan. MEETverso - this is the name of MEET's metaverse - has been designed in a way that recalls MEET's salient graphic, design and architectural features and provides spaces for meeting and education, as well as adaptable paths for exhibitions.
