Irreducible World
3D Modeling | 3D Rendering | 3D Motion Graphics
"Irreducible World" is a short 3D motion graphics video created for Ultra Srl, which requested the transformation of a poster created through artificial intelligence into a three-dimensional environment.
To convey this environment, I chose to use the medium of video: I thought that the environment of the poster could truly be "irreducible." Consequently, I imagined a seemingly small world that, however, proves to be much larger once entered into the poster. The final phase of the video serves as a moment of wonder to surprise the viewer, who initially expected simply a scene set in a fantastical place, but is then provided with a new perspective.
