About Me
I am a young designer who, as a child, fell madly in love with the videogames of the Pokémon and Mario franchises. Since then, my dream is to create videogames that inspire people as much as those two inspired me. As a designer, my goal is to convey a story through my work, and let the user feel a child-like sense of wonder.
